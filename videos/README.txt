This folder is for surprise page videos.

📹 Video Guidelines:

1. Format: MP4 (most compatible)
2. Size: Keep under 50MB for web performance
3. Resolution: 720p or 1080p
4. Orientation: Landscape or square works best
5. Duration: 15-60 seconds recommended

Example Videos:

- thankyou.mp4 (General thank you message)
- birthday.mp4 (Birthday wishes)
- welcome.mp4 (New customer welcome)
- special-offer.mp4 (Limited time promotion)

How to Use:

1. Upload your video to this folder
2. Reference it in data/surprises.json
   
   Example:
   "video": "/videos/thankyou.mp4"

3. Test the surprise page to ensure video plays

Tips:
- Keep videos short and engaging
- Include captions for accessibility
- Test on mobile devices
- Compress videos to reduce file size
- Use good lighting and clear audio
- Add your branding/logo

Compression Tools:
- HandBrake (free, desktop)
- CloudConvert (online)
- Adobe Media Encoder (professional)

Note: Videos are optional for surprise pages.
Discount codes and messages work great on their own!
