How to Order

Browse our Products page and pick your favorite claw clip combo.

Contact us via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, or Facebook Messenger with:

  • Product name (e.g., "Crystal Bloom Combo")

  • Your delivery location

✅ Step 3: Confirm Your Order

We'll confirm availability, pricing, and delivery details with you.

🚚 Step 4: Delivery

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship outside Sri Lanka?

Currently, we deliver only within Sri Lanka. International shipping coming soon!

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash on delivery, bank transfer, or mobile payment apps. We'll discuss options when you place your order.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 2-5 business days depending on your location in Sri Lanka.

Can I return or exchange products?

Yes! We accept returns within 7 days if the product is unused and in original packaging.
